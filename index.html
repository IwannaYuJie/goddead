GODDEAD
I

GODDEAD

death. silence. the end that begins again.

死。寂。终结，亦是开端。

SCROLL
II

THE FALL

the gods did not die.
they simply stopped speaking.

众神并未死去。
他们只是，不再开口。

III

ASHES

we crowned the void.
the void wore it well.

我们为虚空加冕。
虚空，戴得恰如其分。

IV

ETERNAL

everything returns.
even the nothing.

万物归复。
连虚无，亦然。

a requiem for what was never alive.

为从未活过之物，安魂。
