GOD DEAD

AFTER THE LAST AMEN, THE VOID BEGAN TO PRAY.

I

The Empty Throne

神座空置之后，尘埃学会了统治。没有律法的天空下，每一粒浮尘都是自己的造物主。这不是堕落，而是重量的归还——我们终于能够垂直地坠落，并称之为自由。

II

The Unanswered Echo

祈祷不再被接收，但回声仍在走廊里生长。它学会了模仿我们的声音，并将问题反抛回来。在神死后的世界里，唯一真实的神迹是：问出问题时，寂静也学会了倾听。

III

The Cathedral of Silence

最后的教堂没有钟声，只有风穿过语法结构的缝隙。我们在那里举行没有祭品的弥撒，朗诵没有页码的经文。神圣并未消失——它只是变得足够安静，我们终于能听见自己。

The Lectionary

Five readings for the unfaithful.

On the First Night

When the last candle forgot its flame, we understood that darkness was not the enemy. It was the only honest congregation. We sat in the pews of night and listened to the chairs creak like old prayers.

On the Unnamed Saints

Every doubt is a saint in disguise. They wear no halos, only the thin crowns of insomnia. Kneel to them not in worship, but in recognition. They have carried the weight of heaven so the believers could sleep.

On the Taste of Ashes

Incense turned to ash, and ash became our only sacrament. We learned that holiness is a flavor left on the tongue after the word has dissolved. It is bitter, and bitter things keep us awake.

On the Geometry of Loss

God left behind a perfect absence: a circle with no center, a choir with no first voice. We spend our lives measuring the radius of that hole, never noticing that the measuring itself has become our devotion.

On the Last Benediction

May you never find what you have not lost. May your questions outlive every answer. May the silence, when it comes, sound exactly like your own name spoken back to you by no one.

Chronicle of Vanishings

A calendar after the divine exit.

YEAR 0

The heavens closed like an eye. No thunder. Only the sound of a book being shut very gently.

YEAR 7

Churches became libraries of wind. People visited them to hear the architecture remember breathing.

YEAR 23

The first prayer was auctioned as a fossil. A collector bought it and placed it under glass, where it finally achieved the silence it had always wanted.

YEAR 41

Children began to draw gods with blank faces. They explained that absence, too, has a shape if you look long enough.

YEAR 99

No one celebrated the centenary. They simply lit a single candle and watched it argue with the dark, both sides winning.
